हाम्रो बारेमा / About Us

Photo of Proprietor

Srijana Books and Stationery has been serving students, professionals, and families in Chabahil for years. Located beside the historic Charumati Stupa, we are your trusted source for books of every category — from school textbooks and Loksewa preparation to Japanese language books and creative art supplies. We also offer printing, photocopy, and gift wrapping services.
